Manresa has an ancient, diverse and relevant heritage: the medieval walls and churches, the Baroque buildings and the factories and houses of modernist style, the sculptures, the facades of the streets and the squares of the Historic Center. It also has a rich intangible heritage that includes all those festive, musical, traditional and popular manifestations.
